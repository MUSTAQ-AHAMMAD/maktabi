# Maktabi — UI Screenshots

Complete visual reference for every page in the Maktabi Legal Management System.

> **Note:** Screenshots captured at 1440×900 viewport. Pages that fetch data from
> the backend API may show empty states when the backend is not running.

---

## 1. Login Page (`/login`)

![Login Page](screenshots/01-login.png)

The login screen features the Maktabi branding on the left with feature highlights (Case Management, Risk Monitoring, Deadline Tracking, Financial Insights, Team Collaboration). The right panel contains email/password fields and quick-access demo account buttons for all roles.

---

## 2. Dashboard (`/dashboard`)

![Dashboard](screenshots/02-dashboard.png)

The role-based dashboard shows KPI cards (Total Cases, Active Cases, Contracts, Pending Consultations), Financial Exposure summary, and Risk Distribution chart. Data varies by user role.

---

## 3. Litigation — Case List (`/litigation`)

![Litigation Cases](screenshots/03-litigation-list.png)

Browse, search, and filter all litigation cases with columns for case number, type, status, court, risk level, and assigned lawyer. The **+ New Case** button opens the creation form.

---

## 4. Litigation — New Case (`/litigation/new`)

![New Litigation Case](screenshots/04-litigation-new.png)

Multi-field form to register a new litigation case with case number, type, court, assigned lawyer, risk level, financial exposure, filing date, and description.

---

## 5. Investigations — List (`/investigations`)

![Investigations](screenshots/05-investigations-list.png)

HR disciplinary investigation tracker with severity classification, confidentiality controls, and status workflow tracking.

---

## 6. Investigations — New (`/investigations/new`)

![New Investigation](screenshots/06-investigations-new.png)

Create a new investigation with subject, investigation type, severity classification, assigned investigator, and confidentiality settings.

---

## 7. Consultations — List (`/consultations`)

![Consultations](screenshots/07-consultations-list.png)

Legal opinion request management with SLA tracking, priority indicators, and knowledge tagging for efficient legal guidance.

---

## 8. Consultations — New (`/consultations/new`)

![New Consultation](screenshots/08-consultations-new.png)

Submit a new legal consultation request with topic, priority level, requesting department, deadline, and detailed description.

---

## 9. Contracts — List (`/contracts`)

![Contracts](screenshots/09-contracts-list.png)

Full contract lifecycle management — view active contracts, expiry alerts, contract value, and multi-step approval status.

---

## 10. Contracts — New (`/contracts/new`)

![New Contract](screenshots/10-contracts-new.png)

Create a new contract with contracting parties, contract value, start/end dates, type, and approval workflow configuration.

---

## 11. Financial Executions — List (`/financial`)

![Financial](screenshots/11-financial-list.png)

Track financial execution orders with amount tracking, payment recording, and progress monitoring across all legal matters.

---

## 12. Financial Executions — New (`/financial/new`)

![New Financial Record](screenshots/12-financial-new.png)

Create a new financial execution record with execution amount, type classification, linked case reference, and payment schedule details.

---

## 13. Calendar (`/calendar`)

![Calendar](screenshots/13-calendar.png)

Monthly calendar view displaying hearings, contract expiries, investigation deadlines, and other important legal dates. Supports toggle between month grid and list views.

---

## 14. User Directory (`/users`)

![Users](screenshots/14-users.png)

Admin view of all platform users with name, email, role, and status. Supports search, filtering by role, and user management actions.

---

## 15. Brands Management (`/brands`)

![Brands](screenshots/15-brands.png)

Manage organizational brands (IBRAQ, MATCH, FEELIN, SALFA). Each brand can have cases, contracts, and other entities associated with it for multi-brand legal operations.

---

## 16. Notifications (`/notifications`)

![Notifications](screenshots/16-notifications.png)

Notification center with real-time alerts for case updates, approaching deadlines, workflow state changes, and assigned task notifications. Supports click-through navigation to related entities.

---

## 17. Audit Log (`/audit`)

![Audit Log](screenshots/17-audit.png)

Chronological audit trail of all system actions including user activity, data changes, and workflow transitions. Accessible to Admin and CEO roles with filtering and search capabilities.

---

## 18. Profile (`/profile`)

![Profile](screenshots/18-profile.png)

User profile page for viewing and editing personal information, notification preferences, and account settings.
